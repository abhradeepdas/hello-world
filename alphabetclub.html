Our desire to deicate creative and innovative events for those who want to take full of enjoyment and do fun through our events. We are Alphabetian and welcome to our website with lots of love.

We make friendly environment among the people via our events

We organise few social events such as Blood donation camp, Football Tournament and also sparkling concert during our major event "Saraswati puja".

We always ready to make creative things through each and every type of events. We have a passion for people, flair for ideas, and creative bravery that’s at the heart of everything we do. We know it’s the right way to spread our new thought. For that reason from 2011 to 2018 we had been presenting exclusive Saraswati puja pandel. Aprat from that each year we tried to laid bare supreme resemblance to our goddess of knowledge and the arts; Devi Saraswati. We love to see when entire community gather together and creat a friendly environment while enjoying our live concert event during Saraswati puja.
